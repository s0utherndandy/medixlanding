ABOUT MEDIX COLLEGE
The leading healthcare college in Ontario

North American Trade School offers hands-on skills trade training that is career focued. The emphasis of the programs are to enable students to gain employable technical skills so that they can begin their career in their respective industries. Students work in state of the art facilities that emphasize real world programs and solutions. There are campuses in Brampton and London Ontario.
North American Trade Schools offers an extensive career services department with a representative assisting you to become a better job candidate from the moment you begin your program. Your career services representative will help you improve your resume, develop interview skills and guide you through the job search process.
